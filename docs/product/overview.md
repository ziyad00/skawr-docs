# Product Overview

## What is Skawr?

Skawr is Saudi Arabia's first **AI-powered marketplace aggregator** that revolutionizes how consumers discover and compare products across multiple online marketplaces. Instead of visiting dozens of separate websites, users can search once and see results from all major Saudi e-commerce platforms.

## The Problem We Solve

### Current Shopping Pain Points

1. **Fragmented Search Experience**
   - Consumers visit 5-8 different marketplace websites per shopping session
   - Each platform has different search interfaces and capabilities
   - No way to compare prices across platforms easily

2. **Language & Search Barriers**
   - Mixed Arabic/English product listings create search difficulties
   - Typos and variations in product names cause missed results
   - Traditional keyword search misses relevant products

3. **Time-Consuming Price Comparison**
   - Manual checking across platforms takes 20-30 minutes per product
   - No unified view of pricing and availability
   - Missing out on better deals due to incomplete search

4. **Limited Discovery**
   - Platform-specific algorithms show limited product sets
   - Similar products scattered across different sites
   - No cross-platform recommendation engine

## Our Solution

### Unified Search Experience
- **Single Search Interface**: One search box to find products across all major Saudi marketplaces
- **Cross-Platform Results**: Instant access to products from Haraj, Dubizzle, OpenSooq, Mstaml, and more
- **Intelligent Aggregation**: Duplicate detection and result consolidation

### AI-Powered Intelligence
- **Bilingual Search**: Natural search in Arabic and English ("ايفون رخيص" = "cheap iPhone")
- **Semantic Understanding**: Finds products even with typos or partial descriptions
- **Intent Recognition**: Understands user shopping intent beyond exact keywords

### Real-Time Comparison
- **Live Pricing**: Up-to-date prices across all platforms
- **Availability Status**: Real-time stock information
- **Deal Detection**: Automatic identification of best prices and special offers
