## 🎯 Quick Overview

**Skawr** is Saudi Arabia's first AI-powered marketplace aggregator that helps consumers find and compare products across all major online marketplaces in one unified search experience.

### What Makes Us Unique

1. **Bilingual AI Search** - Natural language search in Arabic and English
2. **Cross-Marketplace Coverage** - All major Saudi platforms in one place
3. **Semantic Understanding** - Finds products even with typos or partial descriptions
4. **Real-time Price Comparison** - Live pricing across all sources
5. **Saudi-First Design** - Built specifically for Saudi consumer behavior

### Target Market

- **Primary**: Tech-savvy Saudi consumers (18-45) who shop online regularly
- **Secondary**: Bargain hunters looking for best deals across platforms
- **Tertiary**: Busy professionals who value time-saving solutions

## 📊 Key Metrics

- **Market Size**: $8.2B Saudi e-commerce market (2024)
- **Total Addressable Market**: 15M+ online shoppers in Saudi Arabia
- **Competitive Advantage**: First-mover in unified marketplace search
- **Revenue Potential**: Multiple streams including affiliate, premium, advertising
